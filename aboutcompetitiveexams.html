📝 Competitive Exams

What is it? 🤔

Writing highly difficult national or state-level exams to secure top-tier government jobs or seats in elite colleges. It requires intense focus, zero distractions, and a solid daily routine.

Scope 1: Technical & Engineering (GATE) 🚀

Example: Cracking exams like GATE with a rank like AIR 1-50. This can land you a dream job at a PSU like ISRO, building real space tech!

Scope 2: Civil Services & State Exams 🏛️

Example: Preparing for UPSC or state exams like Group 2 to become a powerful government officer who makes decisions for the public.
