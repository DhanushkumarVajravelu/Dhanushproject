💼 Employment (Jobs)

What is it? 🤔

You use the skills you learned in college to work for a company. You trade your time and skills for a monthly salary.

Scope 1: IT & Software 💻

Example: You clear coding rounds in C, C++, or Java, and get hired by software companies to build apps, websites, or manage databases.

Scope 2: Core Industries 🏭

Example: Working directly with hardware, networking, or computer organization in big tech firms.
