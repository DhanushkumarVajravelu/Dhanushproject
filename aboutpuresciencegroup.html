🔬🌿 Pure Science Group

What is it? 🤔

Do you hate Maths? Then this is for you! 😅 You study only pure sciences: Botany (plants), Zoology (animals), Physics, and Chemistry. Zero Mathematics!

Scope 1: Medical & Allied Health 🏥

Example: You can write NEET and become a Doctor. You can also become a Nurse, a Physiotherapist, or the person who takes X-rays and blood tests in a hospital.

Scope 2: Research & Scientist 🧪

Example: You can do a BSc and MSc in Zoology or Chemistry and become a Scientist. You could discover new animal species or invent new chemicals!

Scope 3: Environmental Science 🌍

Example: You can study the earth and wildlife to protect forests, save endangered tigers, and fight climate change.
