🚀 Entrepreneurship (Startups)

What is it? 🤔

Instead of finding a job, you create jobs. You take a risk, build a company, and sell your own products or services.

Scope 1: Creative Studios & Media 🎬

Example: Starting your own animation studio to produce anime, direct movies, and bring hundreds of written stories to life on the big screen!

Scope 2: Tech Startups 💡

Example: Building a brand new software, app, or AI tool that solves a massive problem, and turning it into a million-dollar business.
