⚕️ Medical Studies

What is it? 🤔

This field is focused on the human body, diseases, and medicines. It requires cracking the NEET exam.

Scope 1: Doctors & Surgeons 🏥

Example: You can do an MBBS to become a doctor who diagnoses illnesses, or become a specialized surgeon performing life-saving operations.

Scope 2: Dentistry & Allied Health 🦷

Example: You can become a Dentist (BDS) to fix teeth, or explore fields like Physiotherapy and Nursing to help patients recover.
