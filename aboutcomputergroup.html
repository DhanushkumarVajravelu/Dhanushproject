💻🧑‍💻 Computer Science Group

What is it? 🤔

You study Computers instead of Biology! You learn coding, Physics, Chemistry, and Maths. Perfect for tech lovers.

Scope 1: Software Engineering 📱

Example: You can build mobile apps like WhatsApp or make cool video games. You will do a B.E/B.Tech in Computer Science (CSE) or IT.

Scope 2: Artificial Intelligence & Data 🤖

Example: You can learn how to make robots think like humans (AI) or become a Data Scientist who predicts the future using huge amounts of computer data.

Scope 3: BCA & BSc Computer Science 🖥️

Example: If you don't want to do 4 years of Engineering, you can do a 3-year degree (BCA) and still get top jobs in big IT companies like TCS or Infosys!
