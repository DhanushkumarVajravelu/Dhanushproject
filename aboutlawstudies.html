👨‍⚖️ Law Studies

What is it? 🤔

Law is the study of the legal system. You learn how to defend people, companies, and rights in court.

Scope 1: Criminal & Civil Lawyer 🏛️

Example: You can fight for justice in courts, helping innocent people get free or solving property disputes between families.

Scope 2: Corporate Law 💼

Example: You can work for huge companies, handling their massive revenues, business contracts, and defending their financial interests legally.
