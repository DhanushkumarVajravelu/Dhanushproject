💰📊 Commerce Group

What is it? 🤔

No Science here! You study how money works, how businesses run, and how to calculate profit and loss (Accounts).

Scope 1: Chartered Accountant (CA) 💼

Example: CAs are the masters of money! Every big company or rich person needs a CA to check their accounts and save them from paying too much tax.

Scope 2: Banking & Finance 🏦

Example: You can do B.Com, write banking exams, and become a Bank Manager at SBI or HDFC. You will handle loans and massive amounts of cash!

Scope 3: Business Management & Law 🏢👨‍⚖️

Example: You can study BBA and then MBA to become a CEO managing a huge company. Or, you can study Law (LLB) and become a Corporate Lawyer fighting cases for businesses!
