🧬➕ Bio-Maths Group (The Master Key)

What is it? 🤔

You study Biology, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. It is called the "Master Key" because it keeps almost ALL college doors open for you!

Scope 1: Medical Field 🩺

Example: If you want to become a Doctor (MBBS) to treat patients or a Dentist (BDS), you need this group to write the NEET exam.

Scope 2: Engineering & Tech ⚙️

Example: Because you have Maths, you can also become an Engineer! You can build bridges (Civil), design cars (Mechanical), or create software (Computer Science).

Scope 3: Agriculture & Pharmacy 🌾💊

Example: You can study BSc Agriculture to help farmers grow better crops, or study B.Pharm to invent and make medicines in a lab!
