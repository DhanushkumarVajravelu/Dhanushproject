🎓 Further Studies (Master's Degree)

What is it? 🤔

Instead of working immediately, you study for 2 more years to get a Master's degree. This makes you a specialist in one specific topic.

Scope 1: M.Tech / M.E. ⚙️

Example: You can study pure Computer Science deeply at top places like IIT Bombay or IIT Madras. You learn advanced concepts that normal graduates don't know.

Scope 2: MBA (Business) 📊

Example: If you want to learn about personal finance, company revenues, and how to manage big teams, you can do an MBA to become a manager or CEO.
